Drug addicts are responsible for the rapid destruction of any society today. The present youth society is rapidly becoming addicted to drugs and they are engaged in various criminal activities. Due to the easy availability of drugs and carelessness, many people are unknowingly becoming addicted to drugs. The horrors of which are slowly being observed in the society. This system allows you to determine if people around you are addicted to drugs. If he is addicted, beware of him and help him to stay away from drugs. Thank you.
